Manage Your Zella Account

Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the Zella app on your device (as shown on our store listing).
  2. Go to Profile → Account, Password and Security.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Alternatively, you may email us at privacy@zella.com from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

What happens when you delete your account?

  • All your personal data (profile, messages, cards, preferences, etc.) is permanently deleted immediately from our systems.

  • We do not keep any information after deletion, except as required by law (which is not applicable for standard user accounts).
